About Us

We are a group of five BS Management Engineering students from the Ateneo de Manila University. Using the skills we've learnt from ITMGT 25.03, we have decided to create a website that helps and accompanies others through their COVID-19 vaccination journeys. It is our hope that our website provides important information and statistics on the COVID-19 scene in the Philippines, as well as assist our users with their queries, important dates, and the like.
